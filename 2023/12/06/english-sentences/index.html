吃惊

He froze,his mouth dropping/hanging open in surprise.-

他呆住了，惊奇地张大了嘴。

At this,his jaw dropped to the ground.-

闻言，他惊得下巴掉在地上。

At the bad news,Mary froze with shock as if rooted to the ground.+

听到这个坏消息，玛丽呆住了，动弹不得。

When Anne placed her favorite toy in the box,a spark of surprise flashed across her borther’s face,he stared at her with wide eyes.+

当安妮把她最喜欢的玩具放进盒子里时，她哥哥的脸上闪过一丝惊奇，他瞪大眼睛看着她。

生气，愤怒

Tom hung his head and said nothing.His father’s color came up,and a heavy rage came over his face like a dark cloud.

汤姆垂头不语，父亲脸色突变，一股怒火像乌云一样笼罩着他的脸。

Never had my father been so angry in all his life with me.

我父亲有生以来从未对我如此生气过。

After a few moments’ silence,he threw his coat on the sofa with annoyance,collapsed into it,and murmured,”What should I do with you?”

I swallowed nervously,licked my lips and looked away.+（随众添加，在紧张分类中）

悲伤，沮丧，孤独

She threw herself onto the bed,and couldn’t help crying bitterly.-

她一头倒在床上，忍不住痛哭。

The next following days,she felt like she was floating in an ocean of sadness,not wanting to do anything.+

接下来几天，她觉得自己在悲伤的海洋里漂浮，什么也不想做。

She locked herself in her room,abandoning herself to great sadness.+

She felt so sad that she found herself sweeping, unable to hold back her tears.+

She dropped her eyes and stared at her feet ,hoping no one would notice how embarrassed she was.

焦虑，担忧，绝望，不耐烦

Fog began creeping into the forest.Everything around them was swallowed by the darkness,undoubtedly leaving them even more helpless.+

树林开始起雾了，他们周围的一切都被黑暗吞噬，这无疑使他们更加无助和绝望。(因环境描写烘托人物的绝望)

Tony stood there helpless,frowning.+

托尼无助地站在那里，皱着眉。

Mary was close to crying with anxiety and fear.+

玛丽既担心又害怕，几乎要哭了。

紧张

The hall crowded with audience made me even more uneasy.My heart was pounding hard,as if it was about to pop.+

I swallowed nervously,licked my lips and looked away.+

温暖，宽慰

Reaching her destination,Alice breathed a sign of relief.+

爱丽丝到达了目的地，她如释重负地松了一口气。

感激，温暖

Tears of appreciation welled up in her eyes as she thanked the driver for his help,but he just smiled warmly.+

Knowing what had happened,Peter’s mother shed tears of joy and gratitude.

自责

I froze there,with regretful tears in my eyes .If only I had not quarreled with Anne. +

后悔

With guilt and regret flowing in my mind,I rushed out to apologize to Anne.+

When my name was called.I could feel butterflies in my stomach.

喜悦

Learning the children’s idea,Mrs.meredith was so glad that she smiled brightly.

得知孩子们的注意，Mrs.Meredith很高兴，她愉快地笑起来(形容词+面部表情)

Hearing what they said,Bernard was so cheerful/delighted that he almost jumped up with tears filling in his eyes.+

听到他们的话，伯纳德高兴得几乎跳起来，他热泪盈眶。(with复合结构)

At the sight of the basket of popcorn ,his parents were wild with joy.

他的父母看到那篮子爆米花，也欣喜若狂。(固定短语)

To his delight,the popcorn was so tasty that is soon sold out.

使他非常开心的是，爆米花很好吃，因此很快就卖光了。(介词短语)

Enormous joy welled up in every corner of the yard.Stars appeared in the sky,twinkling their eyes at them with happiness .+

整个院子喜气洋洋，天上开始出现闪闪的繁星，高兴地向他们眨眼睛。(拟人的修辞手法)

激动，兴奋

On the jounery to the lake,a wave of excitement took hold of/seized/flooded over/swept over Mom.+(无灵主语句，表激动的名词作主语)

在去往湖边的途中，妈妈兴奋的不能自己。

恐惧

A brust of horror held me entirely in its power,and I was unable to move an inch.+(无灵主语句)

我吓呆了不敢动弹。

Waves of fear welled up in/swept over/seized/flooded over us.Our throats tightened and our knees felt weak.+(名词作主语，动作描写)

阵阵恐惧涌上我们的心头。我们喉咙发紧，膝盖发软。

羞愧，窘迫

Hearing that ,the woman sat there,ashamed,feeling her cheeks burning.+

She rose from her chair,and hesitated for a moment,wondering how to hide her inner embarrassment.+

If you have any questions,please turn to the teachers for help.+
